What is Fixed Deposit ?

A fixed deposit (FD) is a type of term investment offered by several banks and NBFCs. These deposits typically offer a higher rate of interest, subject to certain terms and conditions. The amount you deposit in an FD is locked for a predetermined period which can vary between 7 days and 10 years. The interest rate for FDs is fixed at the time of opening the deposit and independent of any fluctuations in the market.
